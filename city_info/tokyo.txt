Tokyo, a city where tradition meets innovation, offers an array of attractions that cater to all interests. Start your journey in **Shibuya**, home to the famous Shibuya Crossing, where you can experience the bustling energy of the city. Nearby, the **Meiji Shrine** provides a serene escape with its lush forested grounds and traditional Shinto architecture[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/). For a taste of modern Tokyo, visit the **Tokyo Skytree**, the tallest tower in the world, offering breathtaking views of the cityscape[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/).

Art and culture enthusiasts will find Tokyo's museums and galleries captivating. The **Tokyo National Museum** in Ueno Park houses an extensive collection of Japanese art and artifacts[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/). The **Mori Art Museum** in Roppongi Hills showcases contemporary art from around the world[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/). For a unique cultural experience, attend a traditional kabuki performance at the **Kabuki-za Theatre** in Ginza[2](https://www.lonelyplanet.com/articles/best-things-to-do-tokyo). Don't miss the **Ghibli Museum** in Mitaka, dedicated to the works of the renowned Studio Ghibli[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/).

Tokyo's culinary scene is a highlight of any visit. Indulge in fresh sushi at the **Tsukiji Outer Market** or savor a bowl of ramen at one of the city's many renowned ramen shops[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/). For a more upscale dining experience, try kaiseki, a traditional multi-course meal, at a restaurant in **Asakusa**[3](https://savorjapan.com/contents/discover-oishii-japan/-tokyo-10-must-try-delicious-restaurants-recommended-by-locals/). Explore the vibrant nightlife in **Shinjuku** and **Roppongi**, where you can find everything from cozy izakayas to high-energy nightclubs[1](https://travel.usnews.com/Tokyo_Japan/Things_To_Do/). Whether you're exploring historic temples or enjoying cutting-edge technology, Tokyo promises an unforgettable vacation.
